About Our Shop

Ever Green Shop is dedicated to bringing nature closer to your home. Located in Vytila, Ernakulam, we specialize in a variety of indoor plants including succulents, bonsai, air-purifying plants, and decorative foliage.

Our plants are handpicked for quality, ensuring they are healthy and ready to thrive in your indoor spaces. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced plant lover, we offer expert guidance and care tips to keep your plants flourishing.
